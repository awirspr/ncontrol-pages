Integration Capabilities

HVAC Equipment

Connect mixed HVAC equipment and controls into a clearer operating view. We support open-protocol and manufacturer-specific environments without forcing a one-brand answer.

Lighting Systems

Automated lighting control synchronized with building automation. Implement occupancy-based lighting, daylight harvesting, and energy-efficient scheduling.

Energy Management

Monitor and optimize energy consumption across all building systems. Identify savings opportunities and implement demand-response strategies to reduce utility costs.

Custom Programming

Build complex automation sequences with graphical programming tools. Create custom logic that matches your building's unique operational requirements.

Data Management

Comprehensive data logging and analysis. Track performance metrics over time to identify trends and optimization opportunities.

Multi-Building Control

Support multiple building locations with common visibility, reporting, and service workflows where the existing systems allow it.
