Lighting Control Capabilities

Occupancy Sensors

Advanced motion and occupancy detection automatically turns lights on when areas are in use and off when unoccupied, reducing energy waste.

Daylight Harvesting

Light sensors measure natural daylight and automatically dim or adjust artificial lighting to maintain comfortable light levels while reducing consumption.

Smart Scheduling

Program lighting schedules that match your building's occupancy patterns. Automatic adjustments for holidays, special events, or seasonal changes.

Remote Management

Support remote or browser-based lighting control where the installed platform and site security model allow it.

Energy Savings

Reduce lighting waste with intelligent automation. Lower utility bills and reduced environmental impact vary by building, controls, and occupancy patterns.

Analytics & Reporting

Track lighting energy usage patterns and identify optimization opportunities. Data-driven insights for continuous improvement.

Plan Smarter Lighting Controls

Our lighting control specialists can design a system that supports lower energy use while improving occupant comfort and safety throughout your facility.
