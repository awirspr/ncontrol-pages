Air Quality Features & Solutions

CO2 Tracking

Track carbon dioxide levels continuously. Automatically adjust ventilation rates based on occupancy and CO2 readings for optimal air quality and energy efficiency.

Humidity Control

Maintain ideal humidity levels between 30-60%. Proper humidity control prevents mold growth, dust mites, and respiratory issues while improving comfort.

Ventilation Optimization

Smart ventilation control adjusts fresh air intake based on occupancy and air quality needs. Reduce energy consumption while maintaining healthy air quality.

Filtration Management

Track filter status and schedule replacements automatically. Ensure optimal filtration efficiency and air cleanliness throughout your building.

Health & Wellness

Support healthier indoor environments by coordinating ventilation, filtration, humidity, and operating schedules around how the building is actually used.

Analytics & Reporting

Review air quality trends and historical data. Understand trends and make informed decisions about your building's air quality systems.
