Key Features & Capabilities

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor building performance metrics in real-time through our intuitive web-based dashboard. Track temperature, humidity, energy consumption, and system status instantly.

Graphical Programming

Easy-to-use graphical programming interface allows customization without complex coding. Adjust automation sequences and control logic to match your building needs.

Smart Alerts

Receive email notifications for equipment failures, temperature deviations, or any system anomalies. Stay informed through practical alarm management and escalation workflows.

Remote Access

Support browser-based or remote access where the platform and site security model allow it, with attention to user permissions and practical operator workflows.

Energy Optimization

Reduce energy consumption with predictive operation and intelligent scheduling. Automatically adjust HVAC settings based on occupancy and weather patterns.

Expert Support

Access experienced technicians who can learn your building, document the system, and support day-to-day operation as needs change.

Modern and Legacy BAS Support

N-Control works across open-protocol and manufacturer-specific controls environments, including but not limited to ABB, Johnson Controls/Metasys, Siemens/Desigo/Apogee, Honeywell, Alerton, Schneider Electric/TAC/Andover, Trane, Carrier, Automated Logic/WebCTRL, Distech, KMC, Delta Controls, Reliable Controls, Lynxspring, Tridium/Niagara, EasyIO, ASI Controls, BACnet, Modbus, LonWorks, N2, MS/TP, and IP-based building networks.
