About N-Control Inc.

Learn more about our company and approach to commercial HVAC and building controls.

Practical Building Automation Support

N-Control Inc. has served the Greater Chicagoland Area for over 60 years with commercial HVAC and building automation support. We combine deep field experience, current controls technology, and personalized service to help facilities operate more reliably and efficiently.

Our work spans commercial properties, institutions, public facilities, specialized spaces, and building teams that need practical HVAC and controls support without being boxed into one manufacturer.
