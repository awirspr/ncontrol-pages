Controls That Start at the Blueprint Stage

N-Control Inc. works with design-build firms to integrate mechanical systems from the ground up. By bringing controls expertise into the project at the design phase, we help teams avoid the costly rework that comes from treating automation as an afterthought.

Our team collaborates directly on HVAC design, system selection, and integrated controls for new construction projects — working alongside architects, engineers, and general contractors to deliver a fully coordinated mechanical system on schedule.

Why Building Controls Matter in Design-Build

In a design-build delivery model, mechanical and controls decisions made early have a disproportionate impact on long-term building performance. Systems that are designed and integrated together — rather than specified and installed separately — are easier to commission, easier to operate, and more likely to perform as intended.

Controls infrastructure that is planned at the schematic design stage can be routed, sized, and coordinated with other building systems in a way that field-retrofitted systems simply cannot match. This coordination reduces conflicts, simplifies commissioning, and gives the building owner a system that is ready to operate from day one.

For design-build teams, having a controls partner at the table also reduces submittals, RFIs, and scheduling friction. N-Control's in-house engineering capability means we can produce documentation, coordinate with BIM models, and respond to design changes without waiting on outside vendors.

What N-Control Brings to Design-Build Projects

From concept through commissioning, our team supports the full project lifecycle.

In-House Engineering

Our engineering team participates in design coordination, producing control schematics, sequences of operation, and submittal documentation directly — reducing back-and-forth with outside consultants.

Full CAD Design

We produce detailed CAD drawings that coordinate with architectural and MEP documents, supporting BIM coordination and reducing field conflicts during installation.

Building Automation Systems

We design and install integrated BAS solutions that give the building operator unified control over HVAC, lighting, and energy from a single interface.

Project Management

Dedicated project managers keep controls scope on schedule and on budget, coordinating with the GC and other trades throughout the construction timeline.

Commissioning Support

We support functional performance testing and commissioning to verify that systems operate per the sequence of operation before the building is handed over.

Post-Occupancy Support

After turnover, N-Control remains available for ongoing service, maintenance, and system optimization as the building enters operation.
